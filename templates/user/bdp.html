How to Obtain Your Health Data

Age

Enter your age in years. This should be your current age as of today.

Menopause

Select "Yes" if you have gone through menopause, and "No" if you have not.

Tumor Size

Select the size of the tumor in centimeters (cm) from 0 to 15 cm. This information can be obtained from a medical examination or imaging test.

Inv-Nodes

Enter the number of involved nodes. This is typically determined by a biopsy or during surgery.

Breast

Select which breast is affected: Right, Left, or Both. This information is usually determined during a medical examination.

Metastasis

Enter the metastasis information if applicable. This refers to the spread of cancer to other parts of the body and is determined through medical imaging or biopsy.

Breast Quadrant

Select the quadrant of the breast where the tumor is located: Upper inner, Upper outer, Lower inner, Lower outer, or Both.

History

Select "Yes" if there is a history of breast cancer in your family, otherwise select "No".
