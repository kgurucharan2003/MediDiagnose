How to Obtain Your Health Data

Age

Enter your age in years. This should be your current age as of today.

Gender

Enter 1 if you are male and 0 if you are female. This is important as liver disease risk can vary between genders.

Total Bilirubin

This value is obtained from a blood test and measures the amount of bilirubin in your blood. It should be in milligrams per deciliter (mg/dL).

Direct Bilirubin

Direct bilirubin is a component of the total bilirubin level and is measured from a blood test. It is also in milligrams per deciliter (mg/dL).

Alkaline Phosphotase

Alkaline phosphotase is an enzyme related to the liver. It is measured in units per liter (U/L) from a blood test.

Alamine Aminotransferase

Alamine aminotransferase is an enzyme found in the liver. It is measured in units per liter (U/L) and can indicate liver damage.

Aspartate Aminotransferase

Aspartate aminotransferase is another liver enzyme. Levels are measured in units per liter (U/L) and help assess liver function.

Total Proteins

Total proteins in the blood include albumin and globulins. Measured in grams per deciliter (g/dL) from a blood test.

ALB Albumin

Albumin is a protein made by the liver. Its level is measured in grams per deciliter (g/dL) and helps assess liver function.

Globulin Ratio

The globulin ratio is the ratio of globulins to albumin in your blood, measured from a blood test. It can indicate liver and kidney function.
