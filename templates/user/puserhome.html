About Us

AI Powered Medical Diagnosis is a cutting-edge web application that utilizes advanced AI and machine learning models to predict the likelihood of various diseases. We focus on providing users with accurate health insights, covering five major disease areas: breast cancer, diabetes, kidney disease, liver disease, and heart disease.

What We Do

Our platform offers two onboarding processes, one for admins and another for users. Users can log in to access our services, manage their profiles, and review their past predictions. Admins have the ability to track all user prediction data and oversee the platform's operations efficiently.

Disease Prediction

We leverage state-of-the-art AI models to predict the likelihood of various diseases, helping users take proactive steps towards better health.

User Monitoring

Users can track their health data over time, manage their profiles, and review their past predictions, ensuring they stay informed about their health.

Admin Oversight

Admins have the tools to monitor all user predictions and manage the platform efficiently, ensuring that everyone receives accurate and timely insights.

Technology Integration

Our platform is built on Django and integrates seamlessly with machine learning models, providing a robust and reliable user experience.

User Support

We provide comprehensive support to ensure users can effectively use the platform and make informed health decisions.
